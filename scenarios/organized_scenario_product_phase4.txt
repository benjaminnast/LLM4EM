Our product “Smart-Heat” consists of the indoor module and the outdoor module. The most important feature for our clients is that SmartHeat is energy-efficient. The outdoor module also consists of two modules: the photovoltaic module and the outdoor air exchange unit. The photovoltaic module has the feature of zero-emission energy production. In addition to Smart-Heat, the company also offers the “maintenance and trouble-shooting” service that includes the annual service, repair (if required), and a service kit. The features of “maintenance and trouble-shooting” are the quick response to error situations, discount prices in case of repairs and personal service technician.
