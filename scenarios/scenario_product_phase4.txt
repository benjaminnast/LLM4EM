The “Smart-Heat” product is a heat pump that consists of two components: the indoor module and the outdoor module. The outdoor module also consists of two modules: the photovoltaic module and the outdoor air exchange unit. Smart-Heat has the feature of being energy-efficient. The photovoltaic module has the feature of zero-emission energy production. In addition to Smart-Heat, the company also offers a service product called “maintenance and trouble-shooting”. This product consists of the service modules “annual service” and “repair”, and of the product “service kit”. The features of “maintenance and trouble-shooting” are quick response to error situations, discount prices in case of repairs and personal service technician.
