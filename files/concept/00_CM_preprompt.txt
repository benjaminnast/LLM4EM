Based on the concept model according to the 4EM method, modelling objects are divided into components in order to specify concepts, attributes and relationships. The aim is to achieve a standardized understanding of relevant terms and entities in the model.

Subdivision of the components:

Concept: Represents essential terms or entities.
Attribute: Describes characteristics of a concept.
Relationship: Describes the connections between concepts, categorized in “binary” relationships, “ACTUAL_ON” relationships and “PART_OF” relationships.
